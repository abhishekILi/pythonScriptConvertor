  1. Purpose

    Electrical hygiene checks are undertaken to ensure electrically safe working environment for personnel in all the compartments onboard ship. Good electrical hygiene onboard ships also ensure defect free and smooth operation of all electrical machineries. The same can be ensured by following correct electrical practices while installation, operation and maintenance of electrical equipment. The following are the guidelines and forms an advisory for the standard of electrical hygiene expected in a ship.

  2. The proforma is divided into two check-off lists for undertaking electrical hygiene checks of different compartments onboard ship, which are as follows:-

    (a) Standard Check-Off List.

    This list consists of checks of all the electrical equipment which are common for all the compartments. These checks are mandatory for all the compartments onboard ship.

    (b) Compartments Specific Check-Off List.

    This list consists of checks of all the electrical equipment which are compartment specific. Compartment specific checks for different types of compartment are mentioned in compartments specific check-off list.

  3. Ocassions of Checks

    4. (a) General

    (b) Surprise Checks

    (c) Equipment Specific

    (d) During the Pre Refit Trials and End Refit Trials

    (e) New construction ships, (Electrical hygiene will be undertaken by WOT)

  4. Procedure

    Checks of compartment are to be undertaken as mentioned below:-

    5. (a) The compartments in list of compartment table against which ‘SL’ is written, for those compartments checks are to be done as per standard check off list only.

    (b) The compartments in list of compartment table against which ‘SL+CS’ is written, for those compartments checks are to be done as per standard check off list and compartment specific check-off list both.

    (c) Complete list of compartment is to be made available for scheduling electrical hygiene checks of all the compartments.

    (d) Standard check off list is required to be filled for all compartments separately.
